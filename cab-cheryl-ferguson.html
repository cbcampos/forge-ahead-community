CAB Member Profile

Cheryl Ferguson

Interim University Archivist at Tuskegee University and a CAB member committed to research that connects directly to community life.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / Cheryl Ferguson
Affiliation

Interim University Archivist, Tuskegee University

Cheryl Ferguson’s commitment to health initiatives is rooted in her personal understanding of diabetes and her dedication to improving health outcomes in her community.

She brings a practical perspective to CAB discussions, emphasizing that research must be understandable, relevant, and useful to the people it is intended to serve.

CAB Perspective
Research should not be something that just sits in a journal. It needs to connect to people’s lives.
Cheryl Ferguson
Contribution to Forge AHEAD

Ferguson helps Forge AHEAD stay focused on the lived experience behind health challenges and on the importance of making research findings useful beyond academic settings.
