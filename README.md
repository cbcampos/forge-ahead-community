# Forge AHEAD Center - Forging Community Sub-Site Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive community-facing sub-site for Forge AHEAD Center at `/community/`.

## Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Color:** UAB Green (#1a5632)
- **Secondary Color:** Campus Green (#90D408)
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Text:** Dark gray (#333333)
- **Tone:** Community-friendly, accessible, warm but professional
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (matching main site)

## Site Structure

### 1. Community Home/Overview (`index.html`)
- Hero section with welcoming imagery
- Mission statement
- Vision statement
- Goals of Community Engagement
- Quick links to all sub-sections
- Call-to-action for getting involved

### 2. Community Engagement Core (`engagement-core.html`)
- Overview of the Community Engagement Core
- Goal and mission statement
- Specific Aims (3 main aims)
- Connection to health equity
- Bi-directional communication emphasis

### 3. Community Advisory Board (`cab.html`)
- Introduction to CAB (12 members, 4 from each state)
- Chair profile: Lauren Hebert-Henderson (LA)
- Member profiles organized by state:
  - Alabama (4 members)
  - Mississippi (4 members)  
  - Louisiana (4 members)
- Role & Responsibilities of CAB
- How to contact CAB

### 4. Community Coalition (`coalition.html`)
- Overview of Community Coalition
- Partner types (CBOs, faith-based, government, etc.)
- SHAPES program explanation
- Membership details
- How to join

### 5. Community Micro-Grant Program (`micro-grants.html`)
- Program overview and purpose
- Eligibility criteria
- Funding priorities
- Application process timeline
- Support structure (monthly meetings, check-ins)
- Success stories/Testimonials placeholder

### 6. Community Resources (`resources.html`)
- Health education materials
- Disease prevention resources
- Links to partner organizations
- Community programs directory
- Research findings for community

### 7. Contact/Get Involved (`contact.html`)
- Contact form
- Get involved options
- Newsletter signup
- Social media links
- Office locations (AL, MS, LA)

## Navigation
- Consistent header with Forge AHEAD logo and navigation
- Breadcrumb navigation on all pages
- Mobile-responsive hamburger menu
