Partner Types

The coalition reflects the full breadth of community collaboration.

Community-based organizations

Groups rooted in neighborhoods, advocacy, food access, wellness, and social support.

Faith and civic partners

Trusted community anchors that help connect residents to information, events, and supports.

Health and public agencies

Clinics, health departments, and local public health entities supporting implementation and outreach.

Academic and technical partners

Organizations that contribute expertise, evaluation, and shared learning while staying accountable to community priorities.

Partner Expectations
  • Participate in relationship-building and planning conversations when relevant.
  • Help identify community needs, assets, and practical implementation realities.
  • Support communication and dissemination in ways that fit the community served.
  • Contribute to shared learning across the coalition whenever possible.
