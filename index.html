Our Mission

To improve health outcomes by partnering with communities to reduce the impact of diabetes, obesity, and hypertension through research, training, outreach, and programs.

Our Vision

A Deep South where everyone — regardless of income or ZIP code — can access the information and care needed to live a healthy life.
