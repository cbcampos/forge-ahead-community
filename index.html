Forging Community

Partnering with communities to improve cardiometabolic health across the Deep South.

Forge AHEAD connects community leaders, researchers, clinicians, and local organizations so that outreach, research, and practical support reflect what people need where they live.

Mission

Improve health outcomes through partnership, collaboration, and community leadership.

Forging Community centers local voice in the design and delivery of health-focused programs, practical tools, and research partnerships that address diabetes, obesity, and hypertension.

Vision

A Deep South where geography and income do not limit access to support.

We are building a connected regional model where communities can access information, trusted partners, and opportunities to shape the work that affects them.
