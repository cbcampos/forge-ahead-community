About Us

The community-focused arm of Forge AHEAD is grounded in the Community Advisory Board.

The Community Engagement Core works through the CAB, community partners, and local relationships to keep community priorities visible and connected to the center’s outreach, resources, and collaboration efforts.

Partnership Model

Forge AHEAD approaches community-engaged research as a long-term relationship, not a one-time consultation.

Our model is built around collaboration between communities and researchers, with feedback and shared benefits flowing in both directions over time.

Community Engagement Model
In Practice
  • Community members help identify priorities, barriers, and opportunities before projects are finalized.
  • Researchers share plans, materials, and findings in ways that are clear and open to response.
  • Feedback is used to improve how projects are designed, explained, delivered, and evaluated.
  • Benefits should flow back to communities through practical resources, stronger programs, useful findings, and lasting relationships.
Mission

Improve health outcomes through partnership, collaboration, and community leadership.

Forging Community centers local voice in the design and delivery of health-focused programs, practical tools, and research partnerships that address diabetes, obesity, and hypertension.

Vision

A Deep South where geography and income do not limit access to support.

Forge AHEAD is working toward a region where communities can access useful information, trusted relationships, and opportunities to help shape healthier futures.

Core Goals

What community engagement supports across Forge AHEAD.

  • Build strong regional relationships across Alabama, Mississippi, and Louisiana.
  • Make community priorities visible during project design and review.
  • Support practical outreach, training, and technical assistance.
  • Improve how findings, tools, and opportunities are shared back out.

Why Community Engagement Matters

Stronger relationships lead to stronger research, clearer communication, and more useful results.

Forge AHEAD works to keep community priorities connected to its public-facing work. Local knowledge helps shape how programs, partnerships, and community information are explained and shared back in useful ways.

What Community Engagement Supports
  • Partnerships with community leaders, organizations, and local institutions across the region
  • Programs and initiatives that are easier to understand, more locally relevant, and better aligned with community needs
  • Resources, updates, and practical tools that are shared back with communities
  • Ongoing opportunities for feedback, learning, and relationship-building
