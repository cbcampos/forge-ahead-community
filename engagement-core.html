About Us

Community engagement is a core part of how Forge AHEAD works.

The Community Engagement Core helps translate research into practical action, supports partner relationships, and makes sure community priorities remain visible in planning, implementation, and evaluation.

Overview / About Us
How We Work

Community voice is built into strategy, not added at the end.

Listen first

Bidirectional communication

Community members, partner organizations, and CAB representatives help identify local priorities, gaps, and opportunities before projects move forward.

Build together

Shared design and review

Research teams and community partners co-shape outreach materials, implementation plans, and practical participation details so the work is understandable and usable.

Translate results

Community-ready outputs

Resources, updates, and impact briefs are organized in plain language so findings and tools can move back into communities, not just into reports.

Core Goals

What the engagement work is designed to support

  • Build strong regional relationships across Alabama, Mississippi, and Louisiana.
  • Make community priorities visible during project design and review.
  • Support practical outreach, training, and technical assistance.
  • Improve how findings, tools, and opportunities are shared back out.

Forge AHEAD's community work spans neighborhood organizations, health systems, academic partners, and local leaders across the Deep South.

Why this matters

Community trust is built when information is clear, expectations are visible, and local knowledge changes the work.

This site is designed to show that community engagement is not a side activity. It is part of how projects are shaped, how relationships are maintained, and how findings return to the people and organizations who helped make the work possible.

What visitors should understand
  • Who Forge AHEAD works with across the region
  • How projects are reviewed and explained to community audiences
  • Where to find practical resources, updates, and opportunities
  • How to share feedback and ask for support or clarification
