Community Advisory Board

Community voices guiding our mission to improve health equity across the Deep South

Community › Community Advisory Board

The Forge AHEAD Center established a Community Advisory Board (CAB) with representatives from each state in our regional partnership (Alabama, Mississippi, and Louisiana). The CAB advises the Community Engagement Core and Center leaders, research and pilot project investigators, and engages in long-term strategic planning to achieve health equity.

"Our communities have the knowledge, passion, and solutions to tackle health challenges—we just need the resources to make it happen."

— CAB Member

Role & Responsibilities

How our CAB members serve the community

📋

Advise Investigators

Provide feedback to investigators with the goal of continuous process improvement through project proposal reviews.

🗣️

Be the Voice

Be the "voice" of community priorities represented by the regional partnership.

🛡️

Advocate

Advocate for study participants, community partners, and other stakeholders.

🔍

Identify Assets & Needs

Identify assets, needs, strengths, and capacity of community partnerships.

🤝

Share Ownership

Share ownership and accountability with the Forge AHEAD Center and our program partners.

📈

Strategic Planning

Engage in long-term strategic planning to achieve health equity across the Deep South.
