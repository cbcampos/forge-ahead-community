Community Advisory Board

Local leaders helping guide Forge AHEAD.

The CAB advises leadership and keeps community priorities visible across the work.

Role & Responsibilities

What the CAB helps shape

  • Review community-facing initiatives and outreach ideas with an eye toward clarity, trust, and local fit.
  • Advocate for study participants, partner organizations, and community stakeholders.
  • Identify community assets, needs, strengths, and implementation challenges.
  • Support long-term planning for health equity across the Deep South.
Regional Representation

Community leadership across Alabama, Mississippi, and Louisiana

The CAB brings together community advocates, nonprofit leaders, health system partners, educators, and local organizations from across the Deep South.

That range of experience helps Forge AHEAD stay grounded in local realities, trusted relationships, and practical community priorities.

Project Review Process

Plain-language review steps for collaborators

1

Submit materials

Share a plain-language overview, target audience, timeline, and any community-facing materials for review.

2

Prepare for discussion

The CAB review process focuses on clarity, fit, accessibility, participant burden, and community value.

3

Use the feedback

Teams revise their materials and plans so the work is easier to understand, safer to join, and better aligned with local context.
