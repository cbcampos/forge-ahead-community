CAB Member Profile

D'Andra Bradford Odom

Louisiana leader in health equity, minority outreach, and community engagement with experience across public health, healthcare access, and statewide partnership building.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / D'Andra Bradford Odom
Background

Health equity, public health, and statewide community engagement

D'Andra Odom is an alumna of Grambling State University and Southern University and A&M College. Public Louisiana profile materials describe her as having more than 20 years of government experience and more than 10 years of public health experience.

She previously served as Minority Outreach Director at the Louisiana Department of Health and later as Health Equity Director for Aetna Better Health Louisiana, where she also established and directed the Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Council.

Member Portrait
Community Engagement

Odom also coordinates the LA CaTS Community Research for Optimal Wellness Network, a statewide effort to strengthen conversations between researchers and communities around activities that improve health outcomes.

Contribution to Forge AHEAD

Her CAB perspective supports Forge AHEAD’s community engagement work by connecting health equity strategy with practical local partnership, outreach, and communication needs.
