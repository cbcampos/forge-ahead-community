CAB Member Profile

Chris Williams

Health Information Manager at Quality of Life Health Services, Inc., and a CAB member who advocates for stronger access in Alabama’s non-urban communities.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / Chris Williams
Affiliation

Health Information Manager, Quality of Life Health Services, Inc.

Chris Williams focuses on ensuring access for non-urban communities with limited healthcare access in Alabama and on expanding participation among people who have historically been less represented in research.

His CAB perspective helps Forge AHEAD think more clearly about what equitable outreach, communication, and participation look like in rural settings.

CAB Perspective
I’m thinking about the rural areas that I go to when I come to the table. These are the people who really need the help that this research could provide.
Chris Williams
Contribution to Forge AHEAD

Williams helps keep rural access and real-world community barriers in focus as Forge AHEAD designs studies, recruitment strategies, and community-facing materials.
