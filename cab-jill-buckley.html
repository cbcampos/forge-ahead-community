CAB Member Profile

Jill Buckley

Executive Director of Stewpot Community Services and a CAB member focused on housing, food security, and the daily conditions that shape health in Jackson.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / Jill Buckley
Affiliation

Executive Director, Stewpot Community Services

Jill Buckley focuses on supporting Jackson’s community by addressing factors such as housing and food security, which have a direct impact on health and quality of life.

She emphasizes the importance of research and solutions shaped by collaboration with the people most affected by those conditions.

CAB Perspective
Researchers can sometimes be removed from the day-to-day realities of the people they’re studying. I think it’s important to bridge that gap.
Jill Buckley
Contribution to Forge AHEAD

Buckley helps ensure that Forge AHEAD’s work reflects the lived realities of communities and stays connected to the practical supports people need every day.
