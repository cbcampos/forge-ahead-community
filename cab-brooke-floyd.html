CAB Member Profile

Brooke Floyd

Co-Director of People’s Advocacy Institute and a CAB member focused on health access, crisis response, and the conditions that shape community well-being.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / Brooke Floyd
Affiliation

Co-Director, People's Advocacy Institute

Brooke Floyd is committed to improving health outcomes for communities with limited healthcare access and for residents in areas where economic conditions create additional barriers to care.

Her work includes crisis response and advancing improvements in the non-medical factors that influence health, making her perspective especially valuable to community-engaged research.

CAB Perspective
The CAB gives us a way to connect what researchers are doing with what our communities need.
Brooke Floyd
Contribution to Forge AHEAD

Floyd helps Forge AHEAD connect research and engagement efforts to the broader realities shaping health in Mississippi communities, including access, trust, and day-to-day living conditions.
