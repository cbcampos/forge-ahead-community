Evon Roquemore

Chief Executive Officer of Bright and a Louisiana healthcare executive focused on behavioral health strategy, workforce development, and stronger community care systems.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / Evon Roquemore
Leadership

Chief Executive Officer, Bright

Evon Roquemore leads strategy for Bright’s healthcare delivery system, including clinical care, technology, workforce development, and community-based mental health programming.

Under her leadership, Bright has developed compliance and verification technology, launched workforce development through community partnerships, and implemented mental health training and treatment models designed for sustained outcomes.

Member Portrait
Education & Service

Roquemore earned a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette and a Master’s degree in Public Administration from the Nelson Mandela School of Public Policy, with a concentration in finance. She also earned Lean and Green Belt Six Sigma training from Villanova University.

She serves in leadership and advisory roles across business, philanthropy, and civic organizations in Baton Rouge.

Contribution to Forge AHEAD

Roquemore brings executive experience in care delivery, policy, and community-centered systems building to CAB conversations about how health initiatives can work more effectively for the people they serve.
