Privacy & Data

A clear placeholder for consent, privacy language, and data-use guidance.

This page gives the prototype a dedicated home for participant-facing privacy information instead of burying that content inside forms or contact pages.

What belongs here

Core privacy topics for launch

  • How website form information is used and routed
  • Where full institutional privacy policies live
  • What consent language appears on participation forms
  • How visitors can ask data-related questions or opt out
Prototype note

Final language still needs owner approval

The planning documents correctly treated privacy as a separate page. This page is ready for final institutional copy once the data/privacy owner and approved language are confirmed.

What community visitors need

Privacy language should answer practical questions, not just satisfy policy requirements.

1

Who receives form information?

State whether a message goes to a general inbox, project team, or designated staff contact.

2

Why is information collected?

Explain whether details are used for follow-up, routing, participation screening, or resource requests.

3

What choices do visitors have?

Clarify whether sharing contact information is optional, how to ask questions, and how to opt out.

4

Where is the full policy?

Link to full institutional privacy guidance while keeping this page readable and direct.
