Community Micro-Grant Recipient

BirthWell Partners

Expanding postpartum care through free doula visits, lactation support, and community-based care for Alabama families.

Visit BirthWell PartnersAll recipients
Overview / Projects / BirthWell Partners
Program Focus

Support for families during the postpartum period, when care gaps can be especially serious.

BirthWell Partners is using Community Micro-Grant support to expand postpartum home visits and lactation services through trained postpartum doulas and breastfeeding specialists. The work focuses on breastfeeding success, maternal mental health, physical recovery, newborn care, and stronger support during the weeks and months after birth.

Why It Matters

Postpartum care should not stop at delivery.

Postpartum careDoula supportLactation servicesMaternal health
What the Project Adds
  • Additional postpartum home visits
  • Lactation support from trained specialists
  • Telehealth options for families in rural communities
  • Training for a new cohort of postpartum doulas and educators
Community Impact

BirthWell’s work addresses a period when many mothers face limited support, especially in places where postpartum doula care is hard to find or too expensive. The project helps build Alabama’s postpartum care workforce while making free support more available to families facing economic and systemic barriers.
