2025 Community Micro-Grant Recipients

Five community-led organizations are currently featured.

These projects focus on practical health support, trusted community relationships, and local solutions shaped by the people they serve.

Mississippi

El Pueblo

Mi Corazon supports Hispanic adults with limited English proficiency as they manage high blood pressure through bilingual check-ins, education, and trusted community support.

Focus
Heart health, bilingual support, and chronic disease management
Community
Hispanic adults and families in Mississippi
Learn more
Mississippi

Edwards Street Fellowship Center

Food Is Medicine connects patients with diabetes and hypertension to fresh produce, nutrition education, and regular health monitoring.

Focus
Food access, diabetes support, and blood pressure management
Community
Clinic patients in Hattiesburg, Mississippi
Learn more
Alabama

BirthWell Partners

BirthWell Partners is expanding postpartum doula visits, lactation services, and community-based postpartum support for Alabama families.

Focus
Postpartum care, lactation support, and maternal health
Community
Families across Alabama in need of postpartum support
Learn more
Alabama

Pinktopps

Pinktopps supports breast cancer survivors through a whole-person wellness model that includes emotional support, nutrition, movement, and peer connection.

Focus
Cancer survivorship, peer support, and long-term wellness
Community
Breast cancer survivors seeking whole-person support
Learn more
Louisiana

MetroMorphosis

MetroMorphosis is turning an underused lot into a community garden that supports fresh food access, neighborhood learning, and resident leadership.

Focus
Food access, gardening education, and neighborhood leadership
Community
Residents in South Baton Rouge
Learn more
