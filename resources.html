Empowering Communities with Knowledge

Forge AHEAD Center is committed to sharing research-based health information with communities across Alabama, Mississippi, and Louisiana.

Our community resources are developed in partnership with community members and are designed to be accessible, culturally relevant, and practical for everyday use.

Research for Communities

We believe research should benefit the communities that make it possible. Our community-facing research summaries translate complex findings into practical information.

What We Share

  • Plain-language summaries of research findings
  • Infographics and visual guides
  • Community-friendly presentations
  • Research highlights from our pilot projects
