CAB Member Profile

KiTani Lemieux

Community educator and CAB member affiliated with Seven Star Academy, with a focus on making health and research information more accessible to the public.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / KiTani Lemieux
Affiliation

Seven Star Academy

KiTani Lemieux serves on the Forge AHEAD Community Advisory Board and is affiliated with Seven Star Academy.

Her CAB perspective is shaped by both biomedical research and community education, with a strong commitment to giving useful information back to the people and communities who make research possible.

Focus Areas

Community education, health literacy, and sharing knowledge in ways that reflect what people want to learn.

Community educationHealth literacyResearch translationTrusted outreach
Statement
As a biomedical researcher, scientific discovery hasn't been shared with the community that supported the work through their tax dollars. As a community educator, I ask community members what they want to learn more about and provide the information on the topic that they request. It gives me joy to give back in this way.
KiTani Lemieux
Contribution to Forge AHEAD

Lemieux helps strengthen Forge AHEAD’s community-facing work by connecting research translation with direct education, listening, and public understanding.
