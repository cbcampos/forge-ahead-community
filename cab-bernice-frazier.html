CAB Member Profile

Bernice Frazier

Chairwoman of the Macon County Health Care Authority and a longtime Alabama leader in community health advocacy, nonprofit leadership, and partnership building.

Overview / Community Advisory Board / Bernice Frazier
Role

Chairwoman, Macon County Health Care Authority

Ms. Bernice L. Frazier is a community leader and public health advocate who serves as Chairwoman of the Macon County Health Care Authority in Tuskegee, Alabama.

With more than 25 years of experience in leadership, nonprofit management, and community advocacy, she has dedicated her career to advancing health equity and strengthening community partnerships.

Community Leadership

Building stronger health partnerships across the Alabama Black Belt.

Health equityCommunity advocacyNonprofit leadershipRegional partnerships
Experience

Ms. Frazier has led initiatives that bring together healthcare providers, universities, and local organizations to address pressing health needs in the Alabama Black Belt.

She also serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of the Southern Christian Leadership Foundation and is widely recognized for her commitment to servant leadership and community empowerment.

CAB Perspective

Her work on the CAB strengthens community-centered decision-making and helps keep local priorities visible as Forge AHEAD develops programs, partnerships, and research collaborations across the region.
